Clive Eric Cussler

Born: July 15th 1931

Clive Cussler is an American adventure novelist and underwater explorer. He is also the founder and chairman of the National Underwater and Marine Agency (NUMA), which has discoverd more than 60 shipwreck sites and numerous other notable underwater wrecks

Major Achievments

  • Founder and chairman of NUMA.
  • Listed on the The New York Times best-seller list over 20 time.
  • Author of over 70 books.
